* Scheme vs Clojure
** define
#+begin_src scheme
(define a 3)
#+end_src
#+begin_src clojure
(def a 3)
#+end_src
** cond
#+begin_src scheme
(cond ((= a 4) 6)
      ((= b 4) (+ 6 7 a))
      (else 25))
#+end_src
#+begin_src clojure
(cond (= a 4) 6
      (= b 4) (+ 6 7 a)
      :else 25)
#+end_src
